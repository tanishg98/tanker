## Timing

- **Tuesday 9am PT** — Show HN. Best window for top of front page.
- **Same day Tuesday 12pm PT** — Twitter thread, LinkedIn.
- **Tuesday + 1 hour stagger** — Reddit posts, one per sub.
- **Tuesday 12:01am PT** — Product Hunt (24-hour window starts at midnight PT).
- **Wednesday** — dev.to / Hashnode blog post (drives sustained traffic).

Don't blast everything at once — staggering captures more of each community's news cycle.

## Account access required from you

Tanker can't post for you. You need to:
- Have a Hacker News account in good standing (>200 karma, ideally).
- Have an X/Twitter account with engagement.
- Have a Product Hunt account; ideally a hunter from your network.
- Be a member of the relevant subreddits (don't post cold).
- Have a dev.to / Hashnode account.

## Pre-launch sanity check

Before you publish:

1. ✅ README is current.
2. ✅ install.sh runs clean on a fresh checkout.
3. ✅ `/cto` runs end-to-end on a small brief — record the GIF.
4. ✅ docs site is live at tanker.dev (or readme.io / GitHub Pages fallback).
5. ✅ Comparisons page has accurate, fair comparisons (especially the MetaGPT one — they have advocates).
6. ✅ Discord server is live, three channels pinned.
7. ✅ `examples/` directory has at least the SaaS MVP transcript filled in with a real run.

If any of the above isn't done, **delay the launch.** A weak launch costs you the only Show HN window your repo gets.
